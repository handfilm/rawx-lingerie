The Structure

Not a mood board. A supply chain with a design language.

RAWx is cut where it's built — Dhaka origin, factory-direct, zero middle markup. Every seam you see on this site is a seam we can trace back to a machine and a name.

The aesthetic is industrial by intention: void black, hazard red, raw calibration lines — the visual language of a spec sheet, not a mood board. We design second-skin architecture first and photograph it second, which is why the archive above is unedited contact-sheet grain rather than retouched campaign work.

RAWx sits inside a wider system — Handfilm builds the content methodology, Hands&Head runs the B2B sourcing engine. If you're a buyer rather than a customer, that engine is one click away.
